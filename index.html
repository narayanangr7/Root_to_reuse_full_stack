Awareness Section

🌍

"Karuvela Maram – A Hidden Danger to Our Lands"

Karuvela (Prosopis Juliflora) maram namma nilathai uyir illa mathurathu. It absorbs groundwater, spoils fertile land, and reduces native plant growth. Let’s protect our soil — remove Karuvela trees and save our agriculture!

♻️

Let Native Trees Grow Again

When Karuvela spreads, native species like Neem, Banyan, and Tamarind can’t survive. Let’s bring back our traditional trees that give us shade, fruits, and life. Plant native — protect biodiversity 🌱

🌱

Join the Movement – Clean Our Villages from Karuvela

This is not just a campaign — it’s a movement for clean, green, and fertile land. Join hands with volunteers, farmers, and nature lovers to remove Karuvela. Together, we can reclaim our land and build a sustainable tomorrow. 💚

🎨

Save Water, Save Soil, Stop Karuvela!

Karuvela trees drink 10x more water than native trees. By cutting them, we restore groundwater and help farmers. Every tree removed is a step toward a greener, water-rich future.
